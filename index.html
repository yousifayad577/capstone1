How Does This Work?

Welcome to Ariel's student achievement platform! Our website is designed to help you keep track of your academic progress and connect with other students who share your interests. With our user-friendly interface, you can easily view your achievements. Our system is designed to give you a clear and accurate picture of your performance so that you can identify areas where you excel and areas where you might need additional support. In addition to tracking your academic progress, Ariel's website also provides opportunities for you to connect with other students who share your interests. We have a variety of interest-based groups that you can join, where you can participate in discussions, share resources, and collaborate on projects. Whether you're interested in art, science, music, or sports, we have a group that's perfect for you. And if you don't see a group that matches your interests, you can create your own! At Ariel, we believe that learning is a collaborative process, and we're committed to helping you connect with others who share your passion for knowledge. So sign up today and start exploring all the amazing opportunities that Ariel's student achievement platform has to offer!

Sign in

Welcome back! To access your profile, please enter your username/email and password in the fields provided above.

Group

Once you've signed in, you can easily see the group you're a part of.

Achievement

To upload your achievement, go to "Achievements," describe it, upload files, select the category, and submit. We'll review and provide feedback.

Connect

Our platform now allows you to chat and email your group members directly. To chat with a member, simply navigate to the "Members" section of your profile, find the member you want to chat with, and click on the "Chat" button next to their name.

Purpose

We are making this website to provide a platform for students to track their academic progress, connect with other students who share similar interests, and collaborate on projects. Our goal is to foster a community of learners who can support each other in achieving their goals and pursuing their passions. By providing tools and resources that help students stay organized, motivated, and engaged in their learning, we hope to empower them to achieve their full potential. We believe that learning is a collaborative process, and our website is designed to facilitate collaboration and knowledge-sharing among students. Ultimately, our aim is to create a space where students can thrive academically and personally, and where they can build meaningful connections with their peers that extend beyond the classroom.
