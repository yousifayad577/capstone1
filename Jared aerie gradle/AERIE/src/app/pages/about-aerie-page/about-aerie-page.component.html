About Aerie

Welcome to Aerie

Aerie is a platform designed for graduate computer science students to connect and collaborate based on their shared interests and specializations. Our mission is to foster a supportive community that encourages knowledge sharing, networking, and project collaboration among like-minded individuals.

Founders

Our team is comprised of 4 passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds in computer science and technology. We believe in the power of collaboration and aim to create a space where graduate students can thrive and excel in their respective fields.

Contact Us

If you have any inquiries or would like to learn more about Aerie, please feel free to reach out to us at info@aerie.com. We are always excited to hear from fellow graduate computer science students and enthusiasts!
