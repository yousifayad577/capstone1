Elevating Graduate Excellence

Our University's Dedication to Graduate Success

At the University of North Texas, we take immense pride in the achievements of our graduate students. Aerie stands as a testament to our commitment to nurturing talent, fostering creativity, and recognizing the exceptional contributions of our graduate scholars. This platform is a reflection of our university's dedication to supporting your academic journey and celebrating your unique accomplishments.

A Personalized Showcase of Your Achievements

Aerie provides you with a personalized platform to showcase your academic accomplishments, research endeavors, publications, awards, and more. This space is yours to illuminate your journey, allowing you to inspire others and be inspired by the outstanding work of your peers. Your achievements matter, and Aerie ensures they receive the recognition they deserve.

Connecting Passionate Minds

Aerie's innovative algorithms are tailored to connect you with peers who share your academic interests. Whether you are exploring the frontiers of science, unraveling the complexities of humanities, engineering groundbreaking solutions, or creating impactful art, Aerie connects you with collaborators who understand the depth and significance of your work. Together, you can explore new horizons and contribute to the global academic discourse.

A Thriving and Inclusive Community

Aerie is more than just a platform; it's a thriving community where ideas flourish, discussions thrive, and friendships blossom. Engage in thought-provoking conversations, participate in specialized forums, and attend virtual events that highlight the diverse talents within our graduate student body. Aerie celebrates diversity, values inclusivity, and provides a space where every graduate student's voice is heard and appreciated.

Join Us in Elevating Excellence

We extend a warm invitation to every graduate student at the University of North Texas to join Aerie and become an integral part of this empowering community. Together, we will elevate the standards of excellence, celebrate achievements, and shape the future of our university and beyond.
